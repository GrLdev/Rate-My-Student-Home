About Us

At Rate My Student Home, we're all about making the search for the perfect student accommodation in Cardiff a breeze. As fellow students, we understand the importance of finding a comfortable and safe place to call home while focusing on studies. Our platform brings together the collective wisdom of Cardiff students, offering real reviews and insights into various student homes and halls.

How it Works

Cardiff students share real reviews on accommodations. Browse ratings from previous tenants to make informed choices and find a place that suits you. All reviews are from verified students from Cardiff University, Cardiff Metropolitian University and the University of South Wales.
